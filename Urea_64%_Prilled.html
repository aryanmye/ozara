Urea 64% Prilled

  • Services | Urea 64% Prilled

Ozara recognizes the importance of efficient and sustainable agricultural practices. Urea 64% Prilled, a nitrogen-rich fertilizer, is a potential tool we're exploring to empower farmers. Its prilled form ensures easy application and fast dissolution, maximizing nutrient availability for crops. The high nitrogen content of Urea 64% Prilled can significantly enhance crop growth and yield, potentially leading to increased agricultural productivity.

    Key Features:

  1. High Nitrogen Content (64%): This translates to a potent fertilizer that can significantly enhance crop growth and yield for farmers Ozar partners with. By providing this readily available source of nitrogen, Ozar can contribute to increased agricultural productivity.
  2. Prilled Form: Urea 64% Prilled is easy to handle and apply due to its uniform granules. This simplifies the application process for farmers, potentially reducing waste and maximizing efficiency, which aligns with Ozar's commitment to supporting sustainable practices.
  3. Fast Dissolution: The prilled form of Urea 64% Prilled dissolves quickly in water, making the nitrogen readily available for plant uptake. This ensures crops can benefit from the nutrients promptly, potentially leading to improved crop health and yield.

    Applications:

    By providing access to this efficient fertilizer, Ozar can indirectly contribute to:
  1. Improved Crop Growth and Yield: The readily available nitrogen in Urea 64% Prilled can significantly enhance the growth and yield of various crops, potentially leading to increased food production and farmer income.
  2. Sustainable Practices: The ease of application and minimal waste associated with Urea 64% Prilled aligns with Ozar's commitment to supporting sustainable agriculture.
  3. Farmer Education: Ozar can play a role in educating farmers about the proper application methods and benefits of Urea 64% Prilled, empowering them to make informed decisions for their crops.pen_spark
