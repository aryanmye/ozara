    Applications:

  1. Refining into Transportation Fuels: The primary application of REBCO is being refined into various transportation fuels like gasoline, diesel, and jet fuel. While its sulfur content requires additional processing steps compared to sweeter crudes, refineries around the world are equipped to handle it.
  2. Feedstock for Petrochemicals: Beyond fuels, REBCO can also be used as a feedstock in the production of various petrochemicals.
